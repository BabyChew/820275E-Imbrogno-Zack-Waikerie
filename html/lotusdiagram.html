A Lotus Diagram is a brainstorming and organization tool. It helps define key concepts or parts of a broader picture. The center of the diagram represents the main idea, with eight surrounding boxes representing additional concepts.

Brainstorming can be a frustrating experience.

The following picture is an example picture of a Lotus Diagram.



First Lotus Diagram

This Lotus Diagram is the First diagram I made, the descriptions of the sections are as follows.

Top Left Section:

- This section primarily focuses on topics related to construction and architecture.
- The central theme is "Architecture," and it connects "Large Buildings," "Sheds," "Small Buildings," "Government Buildings," "Tall Buildings," "Houses," and "Skyscrapers" to this central theme.

These are all elements and aspects of architectural and building-related topics.

Middle Top Section:

- This section revolves around topics associated with firearms and related matters.

- "Firearms" is the central theme, linking "Big Guns," "Power," "Attachments of Firearms," "Small Guns," "Firearm Sport Clubs," "Law," "History," and "Different Countries Firearms."

These categories pertain to various aspects of firearms, their history, and regulations.

Top Right Section:

- This section encompasses technology-related topics, with a focus on "Information Technology."

- "Information Technology" serves as the central theme, connecting "Artificial Intelligence," "Cyber Security," "Computational Science," "Video Games," "E-Business," "Health IT," "Online Retail," and "Internet."

These categories are all related to the field of information technology and its various applications.

Middle Left Section:

- This section covers topics related to electronics and technology.

- The central theme is "Electronics," which ties together "Internet," "Power," "Stationary," "History," "Electronics," "Vehicles," "Dangers," "Machinery," and "Portable."

These categories are related to electronics and technological aspects.

Middle Right Section:

This Lotus Diagram offers a visual representation of how different topics are organized, with central themes connecting related subcategories. It helps to categorize and understand a wide range of subjects.

I followed a specific path within the Information Technology section, ultimately choosing to explore the sub-topic of Artificial Intelligence. This selection led to the organization of categories and subcategories around Artificial Intelligence in the subsequent diagram.


Second Lotus Diagram

Top Left Cell - Mechanical:

- This cell is dedicated to topics related to mechanical engineering and machinery.
- Mechanical engineering deals with the design, analysis, and operation of mechanical systems, including machines, engines, and mechanisms.

Top Middle Cell - Robots::

- This cell focuses on the field of robotics and autonomous machines.
- Robotics encompasses the development and use of robots for various applications, such as manufacturing, healthcare, and automation.

Top Right Cell - Vehicles:

- This cell covers topics related to various types of vehicles, such as cars, aircraft, and ships.
- It may include aspects of vehicle design, technology, and transportation.

Middle Left Cell - Manufacturing:

- This cell pertains to subjects related to manufacturing processes and production systems.
- Manufacturing involves the creation and assembly of products, encompassing various industries and methods.

Middle Cell - Artificial Intelligence:

- This cell represents Artificial Intelligence (AI) and its associated topics.
- AI involves the development of systems and algorithms that can perform tasks that typically require human intelligence, including machine learning and natural language processing.

Bottom Left Cell - Industrial:

- This cell is focused on topics related to industrial processes and systems.
- Industrial topics can include automation, quality control, and optimization within the context of manufacturing and other industrial applications.

Bottom Middle Cell - Medical:

- This cell encompasses subjects related to the field of medicine and healthcare.
- Medical topics cover healthcare technologies, diagnostics, treatment methods, and medical equipment.

Bottom Right Cell - Online:

- This cell concentrates on topics related to the digital world and online technologies.
- It includes aspects of e-commerce, digital marketing, online services, and web-related subjects.

Summary

This Lotus Diagram offers a structured view of various categories, demonstrating how different fields, including mechanical engineering, robotics, artificial intelligence, and more, can be organized within a comprehensive framework. It is a helpful visual representation for understanding the relationships between different areas of study or interest.

Third and Final Lotus Diagram

Once I choose the topic within Artificial Intelligence, I had to choose a question within the topic. Since I choose Video Games as my sub-topic and decided to go with a question around how AI influence Video Games. Once I figured that question out I had to refine the question into a better one.

Top Left Cell - What is the History of AI in Video Games?:

This cell focuses on exploring the historical development and evolution of AI in video games, providing a historical context for the topic.


Top Middle Cell - What game has the most advanced and least advanced AI?:

This cell delves into the comparison of AI sophistication in different video games, with one part seeking the most advanced AI and the other the least advanced AI in gaming.


Top Right Cell - What could the influence of AI be in FPS Video Games?:

This cell concentrates on the potential impact of AI in first-person shooter (FPS) video games, emphasizing a specific genre.


Middle Left Cell - What can AI do In Video Games?:

This cell explores the capabilities and functionalities of AI within video games, shedding light on its versatile role.


Middle Cell - Video games:

The central cell represents the overarching theme of video games, indicating that it serves as the central focus.


Middle Right Cell - How do AI help developers with their game?:

This cell investigates the ways in which AI assists game developers in various aspects of game design, development, and optimization.


Bottom Left Cell - How important are AI in Video Games?:

This cell seeks to assess the significance and impact of AI in the realm of video games, considering its importance from a broader perspective.


Bottom Middle Cell - If AI Never Existed How many Video Games wouldn’t exist?:

This cell delves into the hypothetical scenario of AI's absence and its potential consequences for the existence of various video games.


Bottom Right Cell - What is the use of AI for playing in games?:

This cell investigates the practical applications of AI for in-game playing, emphasizing its role from a player's perspective.


Additional Cell

The additional cell above the top right cell, which refines the initial question into "To what extent does artificial intelligence influence video games?" serves as an overarching research inquiry that connects and relates to all the other questions. It encapsulates the essence of the entire diagram, emphasizing the extent of AI's influence on the world of video games. This structure allows for a comprehensive exploration of the subject and its various dimensions.

Summary

Upon selecting the Artificial Intelligence topic, I proceeded to narrow down my focus by choosing a specific question within this domain. Given that I had chosen Video Games as my sub-topic, I decided to explore the influence of AI on Video Games. Once I had defined this initial question, I further refined it to craft a more precise and detailed research inquiry.

In this Lotus Diagram, nine cells are dedicated to specific questions and aspects of the interaction between artificial intelligence (AI) and video games. The questions span from historical contexts to practical applications, with a central theme of "Video games." Each cell explores a unique facet of this relationship. The additional cell, "To what extent does artificial intelligence influence video games?" situated above the top right cell, acts as the overarching research question, connecting and guiding the investigation of all the other questions. This structured framework offers a comprehensive approach to understanding the role of AI in video games from multiple angles, making it an excellent tool for in-depth exploration and analysis.
